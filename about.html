How We Got Started

Nwokolo Enterprise was founded June of 2003. Nigerian and Greensboro Native, Austine C. Nwokolo found the company. Starting as a small one-man operation, Nwokolo Enterprise has grown to a family business. CEO and CIO, Austine "Toby" Nwokolo brings new definition to the MSP World.
